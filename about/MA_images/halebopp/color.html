Color Figures

Publication of color figures is substantially more expensive than publication of black and white. The LOC has not yet determined whether or not it will be financially possible to print figures in color, although this decision is expected by the beginnning of March.

If it is decided to print color figures, these figures will all be located in a separate set of pages at the end of the volume and the editors and referees will examine each request for a color figure to decide whether or not the use of color justifies the substantial cost. Authors wishing to use color figures must explicitly note this fact in submitting their manuscript. They should also plan the layout of their manuscripts such that, if color is allowed for a particular figure, it is expected at the end of the volume, while if it is not allowed, a suitable black and white figure is also available.
