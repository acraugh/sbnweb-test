First International Meeting

Procedings of the Meeting

The procedings of the First International Conference on Comet Hale-Bopp will be published as special issues of the journal Earth, Moon, and Planets. The editors of the procedings are Hermann Böhnhardt, Mark Kidger, Richard West, and Michael A'Hearn. Since approximately 18 February, the mechanics of the editing is being handled at the University of Maryland. In order to expedite publication, as much of the work as possible is being done electronically, including manuscript submission, refereeing, revision submission, and final transmission to Kluwer. Below are instructions for submitting material electronically and for various other aspects of the refereeing process.

  • Initial submissions - late papers, invited review papers, etc.
  • LaTeX Style files.
  • Use of color.
  • Refereeing guidelines,
  • Final submissions, after addressing concerns of the referees.
