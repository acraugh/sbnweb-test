Catalog of Hubble Space Telescope Small Bodies Observations

This page provides links to Small Bodies related observations made with the Hubble Space Telescope. Because of the way in which individual observers designate their targets, the search programs for HST sometimes find only a subset of the desired observations. The SBN does not normally archive any data from HST, but this page may assist scientists in finding the desired observations in the HST archive. The listing is, as far as we know, complete through 31 May 2017. The list is updated periodically. Note that some of the most recent observations may still be in their proprietary period and thus not downloadable.

The tables below each contain a list of related objects, their classification, and the number of their HST observations. Clicking on an object's name will bring you to a page for that object, listing each observation and providing links to HST observation and HST abstract/proposal pages.

Because of the number of objects, the data have been broken up in to different categories, seen by the links below. Feel free to use these links to get to the catagory that you are looking for, but note that some of these lists are huge. It is recommended that you do a search within the web browser to find the object you are looking for. In most web browsers, to do this you will need to press CTRL-F and type part of the name or designation of the object.
