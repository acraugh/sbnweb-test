SBN Data by Target Type

The term 'small bodies' encompasses a variety of objects. To aid in the search for data, we have listed datasets by their target type. In addition, datasets for specific target objects are also listed.

  • Asteroids (all)
  • Asteroids (that are targets of small body missions)
  • Comets
  • Dust
  • Near Earth Objects
  • TNOs, Centaurs, and Pluto
  • Planetary Satellites
  • Meteorites
  • Meteoroids

Although these are not small bodies, the data was collected as calibration, primary observations, or as comparison and are archived within the Small Bodies Node.

  • Sun, Solar Wind
  • Planets
  • Extra-solar planets
  • Laboratory Results
