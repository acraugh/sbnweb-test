Wirtanen Earth-Based Observations Archive

The SBN in cooperation with ESA has begun archiving Earth-based observations of comet 46P/Wirtanen obtained during its 1996/1997 apparition to support the Rosetta mission. The table below describes the data that the SBN is currently expecting to receive from the Submitters for the archive.
