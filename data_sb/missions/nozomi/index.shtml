Nozomi Mission

Nozomi is an international effort to study the upper atmosphere of Mars, with particular emphasis on its interaction with the solar wind. The Institute of Space and Astronautical Science of Japan (ISAS) is the lead investigator. The spacecraft launched from Kagoshima Space Center, Japan on July 4, 1998.

The Nozomi Mission web site is maintained by ISAS.

The SBN will be archiving data from the Mars Dust Counter (MDC) instrument.
